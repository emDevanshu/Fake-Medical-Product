Code of Conduct
Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and maintainers of the Fake-Medical-Product project pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of:

age

body size

visible or invisible disability

ethnicity

sex characteristics

gender identity and expression

level of experience

education

socio-economic status

nationality

personal appearance

race

religion (or lack thereof)

sexual identity or orientation

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

Our Standards
Examples of behavior that contribute to a positive environment include:

Demonstrating empathy and kindness toward other people

Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences

Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback

Taking responsibility for mistakes and learning from them

Focusing on what is best for the community

Showing patience and helpfulness to newcomers

Enforcement Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing our standards.
They will take appropriate action in response to behavior they deem inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful.

Maintainers have the right and responsibility to:

Remove or edit comments, commits, code, or issues that violate this Code of Conduct

Temporarily or permanently ban contributors who engage in unacceptable behavior

Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:

All project spaces (GitHub issues, pull requests, discussions, documentation, chats, etc.)

Any public space where a member is representing the project or its community

Representation may include:

Posting via an official project account

Participating in events

Acting as an appointed or recognized project representative

Enforcement Guidelines

The maintainers will use the following community impact guidelines when determining consequences for violations.

1. Correction

Community Impact: Minor, one-time inappropriate behavior or poor communication.
Consequence: A private, written warning. Clear guidance on expected behavior.

2. Warning

Community Impact: A violation after a previous warning or continued inappropriate behavior.
Consequence: A formal warning. Continued infractions may result in temporary restrictions.

3. Temporary Ban

Community Impact: Serious violation or repeated inappropriate behavior.
Consequence: A temporary ban from community interactions (issues, PRs, comments, etc.).

4. Permanent Ban

Community Impact: Demonstrating a pattern of harassment or behavior that threatens community safety.
Consequence: Permanent removal from all community spaces.
